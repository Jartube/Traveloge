Guatapé is the most visited place by all foreigners in the department of Antioquia because it represents an important cultural and touristic site in Colombia. You need to know the restaurants and hotels.

Lost City

The Lost City is a perfect place to walk inside the jungle for five days. It is a tour that challenges you mentally and physically.

In the lost city you can breathe the pure, fresh air and meet the indigenous people of the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta and experience their customs and culture.

On this tour you can enjoy swimming in the waterfalls that you will find on the way to The Lost City and walk in one of the most untouched jungles in Colombia. It is an unforgettable experience. you need to know thehotelsand restaurants.
